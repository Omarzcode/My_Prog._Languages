Lesson 1: The Basics

About This Lesson

Welcome to your first lesson! Here, we will cover the absolute essentials of the language. This includes the alphabet, pronunciation of key sounds, and simple greetings like "Hello," "Goodbye," "Thank you," and "You're welcome." Mastering these fundamentals is the first and most important step on your journey to fluency. Replay the video as many times as you need!

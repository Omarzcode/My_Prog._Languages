Lesson 2: Common Phrases

About This Lesson

Building on what we learned in Lesson 1, this lesson introduces you to common phrases used in everyday conversation. You will learn how to ask "How are you?", introduce yourself, and talk about where you are from. These phrases are the building blocks for your first real conversations.
